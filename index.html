Business License

When you are planning to start a business or a company you need to have a business license for it. This helps legalize the process and saves you from any problems that may occur due to not having an official business license. In order to get a business license for the Dubai mainland, you need to fulfill all the educational requirements.

Documentation

Company documentation and process varies from jurisdictions across the UAE for a Dubai mainland license, to Freezone to Offshore. A number of documents need to be translated into English and Arabic, attested, and notarised by DED. Documents mainly comprise of general documents and company licenses.
